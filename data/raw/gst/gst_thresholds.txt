GST Registration Thresholds in India (2026):
--------------------------------------------
- Supply of Goods (Normal States): ₹40 lakh annual turnover
- Supply of Services (Normal States): ₹20 lakh annual turnover

Special Category States:
- Goods & Services (Manipur, Mizoram, Nagaland, Tripura): ₹10 lakh
- Goods (Arunachal, Meghalaya, Sikkim, Uttarakhand, Puducherry, Telangana): ₹20 lakh
- Services (Most other Special States): ₹10 lakh to ₹20 lakh depending on state adoption

Composition Scheme Threshold:
- Goods (Traders/Manufacturers): ₹1.5 crore annual turnover
- Goods (Special Category States & Himachal): ₹75 lakh annual turnover
- Services (Standalone): ₹50 lakh annual turnover
